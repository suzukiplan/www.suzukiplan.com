Battle Hanafuda — Press Kit (English)

Prepared: Feb 10, 2026 09:30 (JST)

SUZUKI PLAN / Steam Next Fest 2026 Feb / Free demo (limited-time online multiplayer)

Overview

Battle Hanafuda is a modern competitive reimagining of the traditional Japanese card game Hanafuda (Koi-Koi) for PC. The game will be featured in Steam Next Fest 2026 Feb. A free demo launches on Feb 12, 2026 at 10:00 JST, and online multiplayer will be available in the free demo for a limited time, from the demo launch until the end of Steam Next Fest (March 2).
